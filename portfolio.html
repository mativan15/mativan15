  • C++ Sales Management Dashboard - Santillana


    Introducing the Santillana Sales Management Dashboard – a tailored solution for educational institutions. Navigate seamlessly through the Catalogue tab, where users can order, sort, and manage inventory. The Orders tab ensures precision for each institution's unique requirements, and the Sales Channel tab offers flexibility with four options and easy receipt generation. Empower Santillana in the educational book sector with a cohesive, user-friendly platform for optimizing stock visibility, order precision, and sales channel management. Elevate your sales strategy effortlessly with the Santillana Sales Management Dashboard.

  • RUNApp - AyniTech


    Introducing "RUNAPP," a groundbreaking educational initiative born out of the NASA Space Apps Challenge 'EMIT for the Future.' This app aims to enlighten users about the impact of minerals and gases on health and communities, using data from the EMIT mission in five cities across Peru. With a visually engaging Landing Page, interactive Main Menu offering news and academic information, and sections like Data and Charts and Academic Information, RUNAPP empowers users to understand complex information in a fun and accessible manner. Designed to expand globally, RUNAPP seeks to democratize NASA's data, fostering an active community and enhancing education in Biology, Chemistry, and Geology. The app's long-term vision includes reaching diverse audiences, from state school students to professionals in the mining industry, ultimately creating a positive impact on science, education, and environmental awareness.

    Designed and programmed by the AyniTech Team, a group formed by college students from the Universidad Católica San Pablo in Arequipa, Peru. In response to the challenge 'EMIT for the Future' by NASA Space Apps, we developed this app in Dart/Flutter, reaching the Top 100 Global and Honorable Mention as Finalists of the hackaton.
